Auction Information

The auction will take place on May 12, 2025, at 2:00 PM in the University Auditorium.

Rules

  • Budget: 10,000 virtual coins per team.
  • Minimum bid: 100 coins.
  • Max 11 players per team + (Alumni).
  • Icon player automatically registers for the team.
  • At most 2 goalkeepers, 3 defenders, 3 midfielders, 3 forwards.
  • If any team runs out of coins:
    • Can purchase 1000 coins at 1tk per coins.
    • Can purchase another 1000 coins at 2tk per coins.
  • Max 2 alumni players can be registered.
    • Auction not required.
    • For every Alumni 1000 special coins will be needed, which costs 1000tk total.
